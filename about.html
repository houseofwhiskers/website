An online store run by
two very particular cats.

House of Whiskers began in a Melbourne kitchen in 2019, when two cats made it abundantly clear that the supermarket aisle wasn't going to cut it.

We're an online-only store, and we like it that way. No rent on a flashy showroom means we can spend the difference where it counts: on a tightly edited range of food, beds, toys and accessories that we'd genuinely buy for our own cats and dogs.

What we believe

Most pet shopping is overwhelming — endless shelves, confusing claims, and prices that change depending on which day you look. We wanted the opposite: a small, considered catalogue where everything has earned its place, delivered to your door with free shipping over $149.

  • Nutrition curated with certified pet dietitians
  • Only products we've tested in our own homes
  • Fair, stable pricing — with up to 10% off every day for members
  • Australian-owned and operated since 2019
